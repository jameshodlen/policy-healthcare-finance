Ohio: 14 Full-Tier Hospitals, But Most Systems Are Missing

Cleveland Clinic, OhioHealth, UC Health absent — Ohio’s biggest systems are invisible.

43 hospitals analyzed · March 2026

Ohio’s 0.2% median is the lowest in the Midwest — driven by massive discovery gaps. OhioHealth, Cleveland Clinic, and UC Health are absent from our pipeline entirely. ProMedica and Aultman provide the state’s best data, while AdventHealth entries again pollute with non-Ohio facilities.

Key Findings

  1. OhioHealth, Cleveland Clinic, UC Health, TriHealth — Ohio’s largest systems — are absent from our discovery pipeline entirely.
  2. ProMedica provides the strongest Ohio disclosure with consistent Full-tier performance across northwest Ohio.
  3. Aultman (Canton) achieves solid transparency — one of the few Ohio systems with parseable chargemasters.
  4. 20 None-tier entries — many are AdventHealth and Mercy facilities misattributed from national txt files.
  5. Only 43 of 192 non-VA hospitals profiled — 22% discovery rate, the worst in the Midwest.
  6. Ohio needs targeted harvesting for Cleveland Clinic, OhioHealth, Kettering Health, and Premier Health.
